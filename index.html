Services

Fittings Supply

Specially shaped connections that serve to connect the pipelines need to be taken into account during the installation and rehabilitation process. FOCO is responsible for supplying these special parts.

Reinforced
Thermoplastic Pipes

RTP serve to withstand higher pressures and temperatures by combining the physical and chemical properties of HDPE with aramid fibers and other compounds.

Internal
Pipeline Rehabilitation

Using the Loose and Tight fit methodologies, a liner is inserted into the corroded pipeline, thus increasing the service life of the pipe at a lower cost.

Pipeline Inspection

Pipeline inspection is a part of the pipeline integrity management for keeping the pipeline in good condition. The rules governing inspection are the pipeline safety regulations. In most cases the pipeline is inspected regularly.

Non-metallic
Reinforcements

In order to avoid metal welds, non-metallic reinforcements (polymers) are used in the external area of the pipeline, allowing a better rehabilitation process.

About Us

FOCO (Flexible Oil Composites). We are a company specialized in the commercialization of Reinforced thermoplastic pipes (RTP), rehabilitation of carbon steel pipelines with polymers and rehabilitation of carbon steel pipelines with polymers. Additionally, we perform the installation of mechanical reinforcements as total integrity for transportation lines.
